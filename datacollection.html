Foxtrick

Download the latest version of the Foxtrick browser extension for Hattrick.

Data collected by Foxtrick

When you submit an error report, the following pieces of data are received by us:

Personally identifiable information (PII)

  • Your IP Address (to allow the server to react to any possible abuse)

Hattrick information

  • Your Manager ID
  • Your Manager Name
  • Your Team ID
  • Your Team Name
  • Your Team's Country
  • The Hattrick page URL you were on when the error/report occured
  • The page you came there from
  • Your Hattrick theme, language, text direction, time zone, date format, currency and legacy settings.

Foxtrick information

  • Your Foxtrick version
  • Your Foxtrick preferences
    • excluding CHPP connection tokens
    • excluding saved message templates
    • excluding saved image or sound data
  • Full Foxtrick event log
  • Somewhat sensitive data about your team may also be included in the above. e. g.:
    • player stamina data
    • saved transfer filters
    • youth player skills
    • your monitored teams

Technical information

  • Error message and stack trace
  • Your browser name and version
  • Your Operating System type and version

Data policy

  • The PII listed above will only be used for the explicitly stated purpose.
  • Other information collected will be used for no other purpose than improving Foxtrick.
  • No data collected by us will be transferred or sold to any third party.
  • All information held relating to you and your teams can be deleted upon request.
